10 Ideas to Increase Nail Clientele

A proposal to enhance visibility, optimize the booking experience, and drive significant growth for nail services at Maestro Utrecht.

1

Improve Service Names and Descriptions

Update the website, Treatwell, and Salonized with detailed service descriptions written by Alessia, as there are currently none. Implement multiple, clear nail categories in Salonized to make services easier to find.

Note: This is an example from a well-performing salon.
2

Improve Nails Booking Page

Overhaul the nails booking page to be more engaging. Merge existing SEO pages and add new content in Dutch, including a section about Alessia's passion, photos of her work, FAQs (veelgestelde vragen), and reviews. The goal is to emulate the structure of top results like lak-salon.nl.

This will make the nails section different from the rest of the site. A full website redesign is more efficient long-term, but this is a more urgent action.
3

Optimize Treatwell Profile

Add nail-related photos to the Treatwell listing and adjust the "Over ons" text from "Hair salon..." to be more inclusive of beauty. The new categories from Idea 1 will also improve visibility. Consider removing the owner's photo to give the salon a more feminine appeal.
4

Create / Improve Google Ad

The current Google Ad's quality is very bad and it needs to be replaced. A new, visually appealing ad must be created with high-quality photos and undergo A/B testing until a high conversion rate is achieved.
5

Create New Social Media Account

I can create and manage a new, beauty-focused Instagram and TikTok account. This will require a 15-20 hour setup to create a theme and strategy, a photoshoot, and an agreement for regular video content from employees. If successful, this can be expanded to the hair salon page.
6

Create Discounts

Implement discounts, either massive (e.g., €5) or dynamically reducing (e.g., decrease by 5% until bookings increase). Once we have results, promote them via Google Profile, flyers, social media, and email. Use themed names like "Back to School," "Autumn Discount," etc.
7

Upsell to Men and Women

During appointments, offer nail services at a reduced price. Create a "one-click" email automation: if a client books an appointment on a day Alessia is working and the time after is free, send an email offering a "Discounted Upgrade" for nails for €10. A single click on a button books the appointment.

This is an example for illustration; the final design will be determined after investigation and discussion.
8

Improve SEO

Pay for articles in local press like indebuurt to improve exposure and SEO. Dedicate 2-3 hours per month specifically to nail SEO, analyzing top-ranking pages and implementing improvements. A monthly report will be provided detailing actions and current rankings.
9

Create an Explainer Tool

Create a simple, interactive explainer with animations and photos to explain services like BIAB, helping clients book immediately. Also, create a printed version to show clients in the salon, which can include a discount code.

This is an example for illustration; the final design will be determined after investigation and discussion.
10

Highlight Top Services

Highlight nail services at the top of the booking page when any woman is booking, so they can quickly add the service as an extra.
